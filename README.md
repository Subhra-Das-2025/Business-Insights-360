📝 **Important Business Terms I have learned :**

1. **Net Sales**: Sales after returns and discounts.
2. 
3. **Gross Price**: Price before discounts

4. **Pre-Invoice Deduction**: Discounts before the invoice is made.
5. 
6. **Post-Invoice Deduction**: Discounts after the invoice is made

7. **Net Invoice Sales**: Sales after all deductions on the invoice.
8. 
9. **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: Cost to make the product.
10. 
11. **Gross Margin**: Sales minus the cost of goods sold.
12.  **Gross Margin %**: Percentage of profit from sales.
    
14. **Net Profit**: Profit after all costs, expenses, and taxes.
15. 
16. **Net Profit %**: Profit percentage after all expenses.
17. 
18. **Market Share %**: Company’s sales compared to the whole market.
19. 
20. **Forecast Accuracy**: How close sales predictions are to actual sales.
21. 
22. **Net Error**: Difference between actual and predicted results.
23. 
24. **Absolute Error**: The exact difference between actual and predicted results.
25. 
26. **Year-to-Date (YTD)**: Performance from the start of the year to now.
27. 
28. **Year-to-Go (YTG)**: Projected performance until the end of the year.
29. 
30. **Benchmark**: Standard to compare performance.
31. 
32. **Customer-Consumer**: Customer buys, consumer uses.
33.
34. **Retailer**: Business selling goods to consumers.
35. 
36. **Direct Selling**: Selling directly to customers, no middlemen.
37. 
38. **Distributor**: Person or company that supplies goods to retailers.
39. 
40. **Brick-and-Mortar**: Physical stores, not online.
41. 
42. **Fiscal Year**: A 12-month period used for financial reporting.
